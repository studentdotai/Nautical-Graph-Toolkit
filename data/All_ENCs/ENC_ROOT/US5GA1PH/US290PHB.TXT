LOCAL DATUM

SOUNDINGS
Soundings refers to the low water plane which corresponds to a discharge at the New Savannah Dam (located further north) of 172.7 cubic meter/6,100 cubic feet per second. The Corps of Engineers project depth is 2.7 meters/9 feet at the established low water planes.

OVERHEAD CLEARANCES
High water clearances refer to a water plane established by a discharge at the New Savannah Dam (located further north) of 1,755.6 cubic meter/62,000 cubic feet per second. Low water clearances refer to a discharge of 172.7 cubic meter/6,100 cubic feet per second.
