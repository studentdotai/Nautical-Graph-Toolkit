The Protected Areas Network (National Marine Sanctuaries of Bikar and Bokak) Regulation, 2024 prohibits any fishing activities (including commercial) within 35 nautical miles of Bikar and Bokak (Taongi) Atolls.  This ENC does not display the full extents of the marine sanctuary.  The following positions make up the entirety of the National Sanctuaries of Bikar and Bokak area:

NSBB1: 11.57461 N 169.857 E
NSBB2: 14.89827 N 168.275 E
NSBB3: 15.32416 N 169.216 E
NSBB4: 12.00652 N 170.797 E
