Vermilion River
The controlling depths were 2.1 meters/7 feet on centerline from the Intracoastal Waterway to the Perry Bridge; thence 1.2 meters/4 feet on centerline to the Broussard Bridge; thence 0.6 meters/2 feet on centerline to the Ambassador Caffery Bridge; thence shoal to bare to the Pinhook Bridge. Use of local knowledge is recommended.
