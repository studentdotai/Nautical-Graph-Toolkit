INTRACOASTAL WATERWAY
The project depth is 3.6 meters/12 feet from New Orleans, Louisiana to Aransas Pass, Texas. Consult the U.S. Army Corps of Engineers for controlling depths and U.S. Coast Guard Local Notice to Mariners for other navigation hazards or restrictions.   Uncharted shoals may exist in areas which have not been recently surveyed. Please report shoals and obstructions at:  https://www.nauticalcharts.noaa.gov/customerservice/assist/ .
