SURVEY DATA:
Survey data has been compiled using IHO S-4 rounding method 0-31 in meters and decimeters and thereafter to the nearest meter.

CAUTION - SUBMARINE PIPELINES and CABLES:
Additional uncharted submarine pipelines and submarine cables may exist within the area of this cell. Not all submarine pipelines and submarine cables are required to be buried, and those that were originally buried may have become exposed. Mariners should use extreme caution when operating vessels in depths of water comparable to their draft in areas where pipelines and cables may exist, and when anchoring, dragging, or trawling.
