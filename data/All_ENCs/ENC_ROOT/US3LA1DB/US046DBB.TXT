CONTROLLING DEPTHS
The controlling depth was 2.4 meters/8 feet for a width of 24.4 meters/80 feet from Loreauville Canal to Dauterive Lake. The channel is marked by red beacons "2" through "90" with intermediate aids not charted. A continuous spoil area, with marked 15.2 meters/50-foot openings every 609.6 meters/2,000 feet, exists to the east and south of the channel.
